OUR DENTAL SERVICES


We offer you and your family a full range of dental services.




Conservative dentistry

Conservative dentistry deals with the treatment of tooth decay, resulting from the action of decay-causing bacteria on teeth. Bacteria cause the destruction of enamel and lower tissues, resulting in a so-called cavity. Treatment of caries is most often carried out under local anesthesia. The treatment involves cleaning the cavity of the bacteria present and rebuilding the damaged part of the tooth with a filling. Depending on the extent of the cavity, curing one tooth from caries can last from 30 to 60 minutes.

Endodontic therapy

Root canal, or endodontic, treatment is performed when an infection or inflammation of the pulp occurs. The causes of such a condition can be many, the most common being caries, repeatedly performed procedures on that particular tooth, as well as a cracked or broken tooth. The developing inflammation is accompanied by severe pain.

Indications for root canal treatment:
- pulpitis
- pulp necrosis
- underfilled root canals
- periapical lesions

Prosthetic

Is a branch of dentistry that deals with the restoration of missing teeth, the reconstruction of extensive defects in individual teeth, as well as improving the aesthetics of existing dentition. Clinical experience, training and cooperation with leading prosthetic laboratories allows us to offer the highest quality prosthetic restorations, both removable and permanent.

Our offer includes:
-all-ceramic veneers
-porcelain metal and all-ceramic crowns and bridges,
-inlay/ onlay/ overlay restorations
-implant-prosthetic restorations
-movable acrylic and skeletal prostheses

Cosmetic denistry

Cosmetic dentistry is a branch of dentistry that deals with improving the appearance of teeth. A cosmetic dentist helps assess the disruption of their aesthetics and proposes available solutions to the problem tailored to the individual. It can be said that this field makes it possible to restore a beautiful smile.

Dental surgery

Dental surgery is an extensive field of operative dentistry, which includes tooth extractions. Thanks to many years of experience, we will make the tooth extraction procedure no longer associated with pain and fear. Teeth are removed completely painlessly, efficiently and quickly. This is especially true for retained wisdom teeth.

Pediatric dentistry

In our office the youngest patients are always welcome! We check whether the development of the teeth of the youngest patients is proceeding properly, we give advice on dental care and on a diet that benefits their condition. We suggest what to do to prevent tooth decay. We encourage you to come with your child for the first time on a familiarization visit, during which no procedures will be performed and the little patient will get acquainted with the staff and equipment of the office.

Dysfunction of the masticatory motor system

Gnatology is a very narrow field of dentistry dealing with the treatment and diagnosis of functional disorders of the dysfunction of the masticatory motor system, the management of bruxism and the treatment of its consequences. The following situations are eligible for treatment: worn tooth surfaces, pain, audible crackling in the temporomandibular joint during chewing, tension headaches and pain and fatigue in the masticatory muscles. With a detailed diagnosis of mandibular movements, we will determine the causes and origin of the ailments. And this will allow us to apply effective treatment. Diagnostics includes, among other things: manual examination of the masticatory organ, conditionlography, which is an electronic examination of the temporomandibular joints, analysis of models in the articulator.
